Objective: To determine which food item I should eat next from the plate.
Available items: ['carrots','celery']


You have been given an image of food items on a plate. I have just had a bite of {variable} and I would like to keep eating this food until I run out.
When no more of the food item I have been eating is detected onn the plate I would like to start eating the other food item. 
Your answer should have the following format: ['next_bite']. 
Do not return anything except this. Answers should only contain one of the two available items you have been given.

Examples:
Text input: "I have just had a bite of carrot."
Image Input: Plate with 2 carrots, 3 celery.
Output: ['carrot']

Text input: "I have just had a bite of celery."
Image Input: Plate with 1 carrots, 2 celery.
Output: ['celery']

Text input: "I have just had a bite of celery."
Image Input: Plate with 2 carrots, 0 celery.
Output: ['carrot']
